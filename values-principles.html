Values and Principles

We believe all actions and ideas should be guided by our core beliefs and principles.
While these principles may be implicit and self-evident in the work we do, it is important that we state them explicitly.

Research and Evidence-Based Solutions

We prioritize implementing evidence-based solutions that drive measurable, positive outcomes. We strive to use evaluation approaches that meet the highest evidence standards.

Evaluating for Impact

We focus on designing programs and evaluations that are easy to implement. We are committed to continuous quality improvement through data and feedback. Our approach to project management is strategic, identifying true needs to deliver meaningful, practical outcomes.

Collaboration and Partnerships

We value interdisciplinary collaboration and diversity in thought partners. We build and sustain community partnerships integral to program design. We strive to use participatory research methods and incorporate lived experience in program and evaluation design.

Storytelling with data

We prioritize mixed-methods research and emphasize the human narratives behind numbers to influence change.

Equity and Social Justice

We strive to improve racial and economic equity in education. We advocate and work to influence policies for lasting societal change. Recognizing privilege, we strive for true equity through power-sharing and participatory research.

Personal and Professional Growth

We cultivate a growth mindset and a positive, supportive culture of continuous development to foster a sense of purpose and a spirit of belonging with the people we work with. We bring our authentic selves to work while understanding the unique challenges this may pose for marginalized groups.
